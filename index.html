Page Description

Hi and welcome to my page! This page helps you visualize the factors that influence life expectancy across various US counties. The counties considered for this visualization are the ones for which the average mean error was <=1 years which were about half the counties (1500 out of 3000). There are two features - feature 1 helps you narrow down on counties based on a specific contributing factor. Feature 2 helps you visualize the factors contributing to a particular county.
